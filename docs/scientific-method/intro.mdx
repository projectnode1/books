# Introduction

We will explore the scientific method and how it has been
able to advance our understanding of the natural world in the
last several centuries through passing of the method from 
teacher to student to teacher as Neil deGrasse Tyson has 
described in the Cosmos.

The idea broadly is cycle of
- Observation: Making observations about the natural world.
- Hypothesis: Form an explanation for these observations.
- Experiment: Test the hypothesis through experiments to find evidence for or against the hypothesis.
- Conclusion: Draw conclusions based on the experimental results and refine the hypothesis as needed.

Interestingly enough, deductive reasoning is considered as 
different from scientific method. Deductive reasoning
involves conclusion based on the premises like axioms/assumptions. While
scientific method is considered to be broader in scope.
